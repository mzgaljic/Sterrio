Search, stream, save and tag YouTube music videos to create your own music collection. 

Sterrio uses a very low amount of storage (perfect for an 8 or 16gb device.)

Key Features:
• Stream videos from YouTube through an organized library.
• Intelligent song tagging.*
• Tweak Sterrio to your taste with themes and various text sizes.
• Create playlists
• iCloud sync, AirPlay and more!

*Feature not available for all videos.
